# GlowGirl.AI Features and Quizzes Guide

## Core Features

GlowGirl.AI is a style guidance platform that helps users discover their most flattering styles, colors, and beauty choices. The application offers several key features:

### 1. Interactive Style Quizzes
- Series of analytical quizzes that assess your natural features and preferences
- Personalized results with tailored recommendations
- Science-backed color theory and style principles

### 2. User Feedback System
- **Suggestions:** Users can suggest new features, quizzes, or improvements
- **Reviews:** Platform for sharing feedback on quizzes and analysis results
- **Issues:** System for reporting bugs or functionality issues

### 3. Modern UI/UX Design
- Responsive layout for both mobile and desktop use
- Beautiful gradient color schemes and animations
- Intuitive navigation and question flow

## Detailed Quiz Breakdown

### 1. Seasonal Color Analysis Quiz

This quiz helps users identify their seasonal color palette — Spring, Summer, Autumn, or Winter — based on skin undertones and natural features.

#### Quiz Flow:
1. **Introduction Page:** Explains color theory and the four seasonal palettes
2. **Question Series:** Analysis of natural coloring
3. **Results Page:** Complete color palette and style recommendations

#### Quiz Questions and Options:

1. **What color are the veins on your wrist?**
   - Blue or purple *(indicates cool undertones)*
   - Green *(indicates warm undertones)*
   - Both blue and green *(indicates neutral undertones)*

2. **How does your skin react to sun exposure?**
   - Burns easily, rarely tans *(indicates Winter)*
   - Burns first, then tans *(indicates Summer)*
   - Tans easily, rarely burns *(indicates Autumn)*
   - Develops a golden tan quickly *(indicates Spring)*

*Note: The actual quiz includes additional questions about eye color, natural hair color, and jewelry preferences for a more accurate assessment.*

#### Possible Results:

1. **Winter**
   - **Description:** Cool undertones with high contrast between hair, skin, and eyes. Crisp, clear, and dramatic coloring.
   - **Best Colors:** Pure White, Black, Navy Blue, Royal Blue, Emerald Green, True Red, Bright Pink
   - **Colors to Avoid:** Orange, Yellow-Green, Warm Brown, Camel, Beige
   - **Best Metals:** Silver, Platinum, White Gold
   - **Makeup Recommendations:**
     - Lips: True Red, Fuchsia, Berry, Pink
     - Eyes: Silver, Charcoal, Navy, Plum
     - Cheeks: Cool Pink, Raspberry, Plum
   - **Celebrity Examples:** Anne Hathaway, Courteney Cox, Lupita Nyong'o, Zooey Deschanel, Lily Collins

2. **Spring**
   - **Description:** Warm undertones with low to medium contrast. Light, bright, and clear coloring.
   - **Best Colors:** Peach, Coral, Light Turquoise, Warm Yellow, Light Green, Warm Pink, Sky Blue
   - **Colors to Avoid:** Black, Dark Brown, Burgundy, Charcoal Gray, Dark Purple
   - **Best Metals:** Gold, Rose Gold, Copper
   - **Makeup Recommendations:**
     - Lips: Coral, Peachy Pink, Light Warm Red, Warm Pink
     - Eyes: Light Gold, Peach, Warm Green, Coral
     - Cheeks: Peach, Apricot, Coral
   - **Celebrity Examples:** Nicole Kidman, Emma Stone, Amy Adams, Drew Barrymore, Taylor Swift

3. **Summer**
   - **Description:** Cool undertones with low contrast. Soft, muted, and delicate coloring.
   - **Best Colors:** Soft Pink, Lavender, Powder Blue, Slate Blue, Sage Green, Mauve, Soft Navy
   - **Colors to Avoid:** Bright Orange, Bright Yellow, Tomato Red, Black, Bright White
   - **Best Metals:** Silver, Platinum, White Gold
   - **Makeup Recommendations:**
     - Lips: Rose, Soft Pink, Mauve, Plum
     - Eyes: Taupe, Soft Gray, Lavender, Slate Blue
     - Cheeks: Soft Rose, Cool Pink, Plum
   - **Celebrity Examples:** Kate Middleton, Jodie Foster, Cate Blanchett, Selena Gomez, Jennifer Aniston

4. **Autumn**
   - **Description:** Warm undertones with medium to low contrast. Rich, warm, and earthy coloring.
   - **Best Colors:** Terracotta, Olive Green, Mustard Yellow, Burnt Orange, Teal, Burgundy, Warm Brown
   - **Colors to Avoid:** Black, Pure White, Pastel Pink, Icy Blue, Bright Fuchsia
   - **Best Metals:** Gold, Bronze, Copper
   - **Makeup Recommendations:**
     - Lips: Terracotta, Brick Red, Warm Nude, Peach
     - Eyes: Bronze, Copper, Forest Green, Warm Browns
     - Cheeks: Peach, Warm Coral, Terracotta
   - **Celebrity Examples:** Jennifer Lopez, Jessica Alba, Beyoncé, Blake Lively, Gisele Bündchen

### 2. Silver vs. Gold Quiz

This quiz helps users determine whether they look better in silver or gold jewelry based on their skin undertones and wardrobe tones.

#### Quiz Flow:
1. **Introduction Page:** Explains how metal tones relate to skin undertones
2. **Question Series:** Analysis of coloring and preferences
3. **Results Page:** Metal recommendations with styling tips

#### Quiz Questions and Options:

1. **Which jewelry tone do you typically gravitate toward?**
   - Silver/platinum/white gold
   - Gold/brass/rose gold
   - I like both equally

2. **When you wear a white t-shirt, does your skin appear:**
   - More yellow or peachy
   - More pink or rosy
   - Neutral, neither yellow nor pink

3. **Which colors flatter you the most?**
   - Bright, clear colors like royal blue, emerald, or fuchsia
   - Earthy, warm colors like olive, rust, or terracotta
   - Soft, muted colors like lavender, powder blue, or sage

#### Possible Results:

1. **Silver/Platinum/White Gold**
   - **Description:** Cool-toned metals that complement cool undertones in the skin.
   - **Best For:** People with pink, rosy, or bluish undertones; those who look best in jewel tones.
   - **Style Tips:** Pair with cool-toned clothing and gemstones like sapphires, amethysts, and emeralds.

2. **Gold/Bronze/Copper**
   - **Description:** Warm-toned metals that complement warm undertones in the skin.
   - **Best For:** People with golden, yellow, or peachy undertones; those who look best in earth tones.
   - **Style Tips:** Pair with warm-toned clothing and gemstones like topaz, citrine, and garnet.

3. **Mixed Metals**
   - **Description:** Both warm and cool metals work well together.
   - **Best For:** People with neutral undertones that don't lean distinctly warm or cool.
   - **Style Tips:** Feel free to mix and match metals for a contemporary look.

### 3. Style Aesthetic Quiz

This quiz helps users discover their personal style aesthetic from cottagecore to y2k, minimalist to streetwear.

#### Quiz Flow:
1. **Introduction Page:** Explains the concept of style aesthetics and personal expression
2. **Question Series:** Analysis of preferences and lifestyle
3. **Results Page:** Style aesthetic identification with outfit ideas

#### Quiz Questions and Options:

1. **Which words best describe your ideal outfit?**
   - Comfortable, simple, and timeless
   - Bold, expressive, and trendy
   - Romantic, detailed, and feminine
   - Edgy, cool, and statement-making

2. **What do you prioritize when choosing clothes?**
   - Quality and longevity
   - Making a statement or expressing myself
   - Comfort and practicality
   - Following current trends

3. **Which era's style do you most admire?**
   - 1990s/2000s
   - 1950s/1960s
   - 1970s/1980s
   - Contemporary/futuristic

#### Possible Results:

1. **Minimalist**
   - **Description:** Clean lines, neutral colors, and high-quality basics.
   - **Key Pieces:** White button-down, high-quality jeans, loafers, structured blazer.
   - **Style Icons:** Angelina Jolie, Gwyneth Paltrow, Calvin Klein aesthetics.

2. **Cottagecore**
   - **Description:** Romantic, pastoral, inspired by rural life and simplicity.
   - **Key Pieces:** Floral dresses, straw hats, lace details, natural fabrics.
   - **Style Icons:** Taylor Swift (folklore era), Jeanne Damas.

3. **Y2K/Nostalgic**
   - **Description:** Playful, kitschy, inspired by late 90s/early 2000s fashion.
   - **Key Pieces:** Mini skirts, butterfly motifs, platform shoes, crop tops.
   - **Style Icons:** Bella Hadid, Dua Lipa, early 2000s Britney Spears.

4. **Streetwear**
   - **Description:** Urban, comfortable, sporty with artistic elements.
   - **Key Pieces:** Hoodies, sneakers, graphic tees, oversized silhouettes.
   - **Style Icons:** Rihanna, Billie Eilish, Virgil Abloh aesthetics.

### 4. Makeup Style Quiz

This quiz helps users match with the makeup style that best suits their features and personality.

#### Quiz Flow:
1. **Introduction Page:** Explains how makeup can enhance natural features
2. **Question Series:** Analysis of preferences and features
3. **Results Page:** Makeup style recommendations with product types

#### Quiz Questions and Options:

1. **What's your makeup comfort level?**
   - Minimal/natural (just the basics)
   - Moderate (enjoy experimenting occasionally)
   - Full glam (love creating dramatic looks)
   - No makeup (prefer the bare face look)

2. **Which feature do you most like to emphasize?**
   - Eyes
   - Lips
   - Skin/complexion
   - Cheeks

3. **What's your skin type?**
   - Oily
   - Dry
   - Combination
   - Normal

#### Possible Results:

1. **Natural/No-Makeup Makeup**
   - **Description:** Enhances natural features without looking like wearing makeup.
   - **Key Products:** Tinted moisturizer, cream blush, mascara, tinted lip balm.
   - **Best For:** Daily wear, office environments, or makeup beginners.

2. **Soft Glam**
   - **Description:** Defined features with blended textures and medium coverage.
   - **Key Products:** Medium coverage foundation, neutral eyeshadows, defined brows.
   - **Best For:** Everyday elegance, date nights, social gatherings.

3. **Bold/Dramatic**
   - **Description:** Statement makeup with intense colors and high contrast.
   - **Key Products:** Full coverage foundation, vibrant eyeshadows, liquid liner, bold lipstick.
   - **Best For:** Special occasions, photoshoots, creative expression.

4. **Fresh/Dewy**
   - **Description:** Luminous, glowing skin with minimal color.
   - **Key Products:** Illuminating primer, cream products, highlighter, glossy lips.
   - **Best For:** Youthful appearance, summer looks, casual settings.

### 5. Hair Color Analysis

This quiz helps users get suggestions for flattering hair colors based on skin tone, eye color, and personal vibe.

#### Quiz Flow:
1. **Introduction Page:** Explains how hair color can complement natural features
2. **Question Series:** Analysis of coloring and preferences
3. **Results Page:** Hair color recommendations with maintenance tips

#### Quiz Questions and Options:

1. **What is your natural hair color?**
   - Black or dark brown
   - Medium to light brown
   - Blonde
   - Red or auburn

2. **What is your eye color?**
   - Brown
   - Blue
   - Green
   - Hazel

3. **What is your skin undertone?**
   - Warm (golden, yellow, peachy)
   - Cool (pink, rosy, bluish)
   - Neutral (balanced, neither distinctly warm nor cool)
   - Not sure

#### Possible Results:

1. **Cool Tones (Ash, Platinum, Silver)**
   - **Description:** Hair colors with blue/violet undertones.
   - **Best For:** Cool skin undertones, creating contrast with warm features.
   - **Maintenance Level:** High (requires purple shampoo and regular toning).

2. **Warm Tones (Golden, Copper, Caramel)**
   - **Description:** Hair colors with red/orange/yellow undertones.
   - **Best For:** Warm skin undertones, enhancing warmth in features.
   - **Maintenance Level:** Medium (requires color-depositing products).

3. **Rich Tones (Chocolate, Burgundy, Espresso)**
   - **Description:** Deep, saturated colors with dimension.
   - **Best For:** Adding richness and depth to natural coloring.
   - **Maintenance Level:** Medium-low (fades gradually, less visible roots).

4. **Natural-Looking Highlights/Balayage**
   - **Description:** Subtle dimension that enhances natural color.
   - **Best For:** Low-maintenance approach, natural enhancement.
   - **Maintenance Level:** Low (grows out naturally with minimal visible roots).

### 6. Hairstyle Analysis

This quiz helps users find hairstyles that complement both their face shape and current outfit or occasion.

#### Quiz Flow:
1. **Introduction Page:** Explains how hairstyles can balance facial features
2. **Question Series:** Analysis of face shape and preferences
3. **Results Page:** Hairstyle recommendations for different occasions

#### Quiz Questions and Options:

1. **What is your face shape?**
   - Round
   - Oval
   - Square
   - Heart
   - Diamond
   - Rectangular/Oblong

2. **What is your hair texture?**
   - Straight
   - Wavy
   - Curly
   - Coily

3. **How much time do you typically spend styling your hair?**
   - Less than 5 minutes
   - 5-15 minutes
   - 15-30 minutes
   - 30+ minutes

#### Possible Results:

1. **Face-Framing Layers**
   - **Description:** Versatile cut that highlights cheekbones and eyes.
   - **Best For:** Almost all face shapes, especially round and square faces.
   - **Styling Options:** Can be worn sleek or with waves for different looks.

2. **Textured Bob**
   - **Description:** Classic short to medium-length cut with modern texture.
   - **Best For:** Oval, heart, and diamond face shapes.
   - **Styling Options:** Can be worn sleek for formal occasions or tousled for casual settings.

3. **Long Layers**
   - **Description:** Length with subtle layers for movement and dimension.
   - **Best For:** Heart, oval, and rectangular face shapes.
   - **Styling Options:** Ponytails, braids, waves, or sleek blowouts.

4. **Curly Shag**
   - **Description:** Layered cut that enhances natural texture and volume.
   - **Best For:** Oval, diamond, and heart-shaped faces.
   - **Styling Options:** Can be diffused for maximum volume or defined for sculpted curls.
